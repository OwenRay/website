Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scripture Roots?

Scripture Roots is a Bible study app that helps you understand the original meaning behind Scripture by showing Hebrew and Greek root words, Strong’s Concordance definitions, and deeper word connections throughout the Bible.

How do I use Scripture Roots?

Simply open a passage and tap on a word to explore its Hebrew or Greek root, definition, pronunciation, and related uses across Scripture. You can also search by meaning to study deeper themes and context.

Is Scripture Roots free to use?

Yes, Scripture Roots is free to download and use.

Which Bible translations are included?

Scripture Roots uses public domain Bible texts where available. Translation availability may vary depending on the version included in the app.

Who is Scripture Roots for?

Scripture Roots is designed for anyone who wants to study the Bible more deeply, whether you are new to Bible study, doing daily devotionals, or preparing sermons and lessons.
